About Our Guild

Our Story

Founded in the dusty corners of The Crossroads tavern, the Bloodsworn has grown from a small band of adventurers into one of the most feared guilds across Azeroth. Our tavern serves as both headquarters and home, where strategies are planned over strong ale and tales of victory are shared around crackling fires.

We've weathered the storms of Molten Core, conquered the depths of Blackwing Lair, and emerged victorious from the ancient temple of Ahn'Qiraj. Now, as new challenges arise, we seek fresh blood to strengthen our ranks.

Our Values

Honor. Loyalty. Victory. The Bloodsworn values skill and dedication, but above all, we value those who understand that true strength comes from fighting as one. Whether you're a seasoned raider or new to the battlefield, if you bring determination and a thirst for glory, there's a place for you at our table.
